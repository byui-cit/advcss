Self Enroll in a Group

This week you need to pick a group to be part of for the semester. Your group should be 2-3 people and the group should have a balance of strengths (design and development). To pick a group:

  1. Click on "More Tools" from the blue menu bar above. Do this in a new tab or window, otherwise you will lose these instructions.
  2. Select "Groups" from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on "View Available Groups" button.
  4. In the "Semester Groups" category you will see all of the available topics/groups.
  5. Select the group you would like to be part of by clicking "Join" at the right.
  6. If a group is already full you will not be able to join it and you will need to sign up for a different group.
  7. You can click on the fractions underneath the membership column to view which of your peers have also signed up for that group.
