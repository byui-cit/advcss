Next Steps with Foundation

This week I would like you to complete Next Steps in Foundation course from Sitepoint. The course is quite well done (I especially like the prototyping sections), but it was made for Foundation v5 and a few things changed.

Watch for these things:

  • Foundation 5 used a directory called stylesheets for it's final css. In Foundation 6 this is simply css
  • I would probably just skip lesson 2. It walks through the basics of Sass, then installing the Sass version of Foundation. The installation instructions have changed. If you completed the Foundation activity from last week you should already have it running.
  • You may find a few mentions of Compass. that was part of the old installation...if you see compass watch translate it to npm start
  • The last major thing is in the lesson on Optimization. That has changed as well. Before if you wanted everything you uncommented the @import 'foundation' line and left everything else commented. To get just specific parts you commented out @import 'foundation' and then uncommented the parts you need. Now you must leave the @import 'foundation' uncommented always and just adjust the @includes below.
