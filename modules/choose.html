WDD 331

Wildcard Module: Choose your own adventure

Hours: 3

This course cannot possibly cover all of the topics in CSS in a semester. The first outcome of the course talks about becoming better at learning how to learn. In that spirit this module allows you to choose a topic not provided in the existing modules and learn about it. You may complete this module up to three times, once for Tools, once for layout, and once for CSS.

Prepare

Begin by finding 2-3 good resources about your topic. These can be blog posts, articles, tutorials, or videos. Study your resources until you feel you have gained an understanding of the topic. Spend 1-2 hours with this study.

Ponder/Prove

Find or design a small project to help you practice and apply the knowledge/skills you gained above. Create a codepen demonstrating what you have accomplished. Your project should take 1-2 hours.
