WDD 331

Wildcard Module: Choose your own adventure

Hours: 6

This course cannot possibly cover all of the topics in CSS in a semester. The first outcome of the course talks about becoming better at learning how to learn. In that spirit this module allows you to choose a topic not provided in the existing modules and learn about it.

Prepare

Begin by finding 2-3 good resources about your topic. These can be blog posts, articles, tutorials, or videos. Study your resources until you feel you have gained an understanding of the topic. Spend 1-3 hours with this study.

Ponder/Prove/Objective:

Find or design a small project to help you practice and apply the knowledge/skills you gained above. Create a codepen demonstrating what you have accomplished. Your project should take 3-4 hours.
