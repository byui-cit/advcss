Repeating Students Weeks 5-12

Description

If you are taking the course for the second time, the way you spend weeks 5-12 can vary. You have a few options:

  1. First, if the class chooses a module which you have not completed you should work on that module with the rest of the class.
  2. If the class chooses a module for the week that you have already completed, but you feel that you did not gain the mastery of that topic that you wanted the first time around, then you have the option of studying that topic on your own to learn more. You should make a specific goal or two, and should decide on an activity that you will complete and turn in to the instructor to show that you have met your goals.
  3. You also have the option of choosing a different module from the class if they pick one you have already done.
  4. Finally, you could work similarly to w1-4, where you find a site with something on it that you are interested in and spend your time learning how to replicate that thing.

You should communicate your decision on how you will spend your time each week to the instructor before the week begins!.

You will report on your efforts for each week using the same reflection as the rest of the class.
