Advanced CSS

WDD 331

Required Module: Zen Garden, Finishing up

Hours: 6

Prepare

Make sure to have completed the Zen Garden part 1 module before this.

Ponder

Review the project requirements:

  1. Your Zen Garden version should follow a theme.
  2. You need to follow the Zen Garden guidelines. They are all outlined in the content on the page (You read that right?) You must have rights to any media you use. I would actually recommend that you try and produce all your graphics with either CSS or SVG. (This means don’t spend a lot of time creating art. Keep it simple). Remember also that you are not allowed to modify the html…so any imagery you add has to be in the css.
  3. The Zen Garden guidelines do not require responsiveness, I do. Your final product must be responsive.
  4. This should represent your best work and should try to incorporate many of the topics studied the semester. A minimal effort here will not get top scores.
  5. All the CSS must be your own work. Libraries are great at times…but this is not one of them.

Prove

Complete your Zen Garden Site. This is not due until the end of the course and you will have time during the last week of the course to work on this. If you do nothing until then, however, you will struggle to produce quality work. Coordinate with your teams to make some progress each week.

Once done the group should submit the following:

  1. The names of all members of the group,
  2. a description of the theme you have chosen and ideas about how you will incorporate it into your design,
  3. a mention of which topics the group included in their project from the course modules,
  4. either a URL where your project can be seen or an archive of your files.

Each group should also be prepared to present their project. Your instructor will let you know if that will be in synchronous (meeting in class or online) or asynchonous (a video).

Grading

  • Final project follows the Zen Garden guidelines (read the content of your page if you have forgotten what these are).
  • Final project makes good use of the proposed theme and shows attention to detail.
  • Project includes examples of many of the topics from the modules available for the course.
  • Final project is responsive.
  • SCSS is used and the file is provided.
  • Compiled css is valid.
